## 3. Sentiment Summary

### Dominant Negative Themes
1. **Price / Value** — Overwhelmingly the #1 complaint. Customers repeatedly call the food "overpriced" relative to portion size and quality. UK customers especially note £35–43 for two burgers and a drink.
2. **Declining Quality** — Multiple reviewers describe a downward trend: "getting worse and worse," soggy/greasy buns, lukewarm fries, small portions, and inconsistent meat quality.
3. **Slow Service / Long Waits** — Wait times of 20–30+ minutes for simple orders are frequently cited.
4. **Cleanliness** — Several reports of dirty kitchen areas, rubbish piled up, and unsanitary delivery packaging.
5. **Staff Issues** — Reports of inattentive, slow, or rude staff; complaints about unresponsive corporate customer service channels.

### Dominant Positive Themes
1. **Burger Taste (when good)** — Fans describe ShackBurger as a genuinely good burger worth paying extra for over standard fast food.
2. **Shakes / Drinks** — Milkshakes are consistently praised as "some of the best in the industry" (CNBC).
3. **Clean/Modern Locations** — Select locations (Victoria London, Burlington MA, Gatwick) earn praise for cleanliness and friendly staff.
4. **Ordering System** — Digital ordering kiosks are appreciated for speed and convenience.
5. **Gluten-Free Options** — Noted positively by at least one reviewer.
